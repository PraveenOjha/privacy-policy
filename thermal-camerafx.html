2. App Performance & Metrics (Non-Personal Data)

Our app collects non-personal data necessary for maintaining and improving the app's quality and stability. This data includes:

  • App Performance & Crashes: Data regarding app stability, errors, and crashes, which helps us fix bugs and improve user experience.
  • In-App Purchases / Unlocks: Key metrics regarding purchases. This data is stored on Google's secure servers for billing and transaction validation purposes and cannot be deleted by us.

Data regarding app performance (excluding in-app purchase data) is **automatically deleted after 180 days**.

3. Advertising (AdMob)

This application uses the **Google AdMob** service to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data to provide relevant and personalized ads. For more information on how Google uses data, please see Google's privacy policy.

The following network permissions are strictly used to facilitate advertisements:

  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
  • android.permission.INTERNET

4. App Permissions Explained

The following permissions are required by the app to function as intended:

  • android.permission.CAMERA - For taking photos and videos with the thermal effect.
  • android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO - For recording audio when capturing a video.
  • android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - To display existing photos and videos in the built-in gallery.
  • android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE - To save new photos and videos captured by the app.
  • android.permission.WAKE_LOCK - To prevent the device from sleeping while recording videos.
  • com.android.vending.BILLING - For processing secure in-app purchase upgrades through the Google Play Store.
